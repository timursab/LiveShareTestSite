Tripaholic 
The Purpose of Tripaholic is to provide you with a detailed trip for the city of your choice. Tripaholic accomplishes this task by listing nearby monuments, restaurants, and museums to bring your next trip to life. 

Tripaholic works like a shopping list that lists all the famous attractions of that particular town/area. You can scroll through this interactive list generated by our revolutionary algorithm.

Tripaholic will source its data from API’s such as Google Maps
Hotels.com. This information will be fed to our algorithm that will sort the data according to our users preferences. 

Your trip will be planned according to your budget.
Your budget is an important part of the app ass it will determine the places you visit.









Algorithm steps:
1-Ask input from user about travel destination.
2-Search hotels around the destination using hotel API’s.
3-List those hotels to the user.
4-Search tourist attractions around the destination using google maps API.
5-Ask user to select those attractions that the user would like to visit.
6-Search for restaurants,ask user to select one.
7-List all selected locations with order, show travel times between those locations using google maps API.
