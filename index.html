 Trip Generator


  Sometimes in life we get bored and for those times tourism is the solution, but planning your trip could take your time and money. With Trip Generator you can generate a weekend trip with our revolutionary algorithm for free and live life to its fullest extent.

PNG Here

  The Purpose of Tripaholic is to provide you with a detailed trip for the city of your choice. Tripaholic accomplishes this task by listing nearby monuments, restaurants, and museums to bring your next trip to life. Tripaholic works like a shopping list that lists all the famous attractions of that particular town/area. You can scroll through this interactive list generated by our revolutionary algorithm. Tripaholic will source its data from API’s such as Google Maps Hotels.com. This information will be fed to our algorithm that will sort the data according to our users preferences.

  Your trip will be planned according to your budget. Your budget is an important part of the app as it will determine the places you visit.
